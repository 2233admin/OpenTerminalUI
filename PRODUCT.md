# Product

## Register

product

## Users

OpenTerminalUI serves traders, individual investors, researchers, and quant teams who need to scan markets, compare securities, and move quickly from an idea to a chart, portfolio decision, or backtest.

## Product Purpose

OpenTerminalUI is a self-hosted financial terminal that combines market data, charting, screeners, portfolio analytics, derivatives workflows, and research tools in one browser-based workspace. Success means users can find investable ideas, understand why a security qualifies, and take the next action without fighting the interface.

## Brand Personality

Clear, analytical, and professional. The product should feel powerful without looking crowded, with Tickertape-like clarity for stock discovery and terminal-grade depth available when users ask for it.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered Bloomberg-style density on discovery pages, repeated card mosaics, overloaded filter panels, low-contrast gray text, and layouts where every metric competes for attention. The screener should not look like a debug console or a spreadsheet dumped into a browser.

## Design Principles

1. Progressive depth: show the few decisions that matter first, then reveal advanced controls on demand.
2. Scan before configure: users should understand the current screen, result count, and ranking logic before editing filters.
3. Explain the result: every ranked row should make the reason for inclusion easy to see.
4. Respect data density: tables stay efficient, but hierarchy, grouping, and whitespace prevent fatigue.
5. Fast next action: chart, inspect, save, compare, and backtest paths should be obvious from the result set.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-usable filter and table controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion fallbacks, and color-independent market state labels.
